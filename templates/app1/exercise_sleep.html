Sleep tips

  • Keep a consistent sleep and wake time (even on the weekends)
  • Limit screen time for 60 minutes prior to going to bed; dim the lights; meditate
  • Keep the room cool, dark, and quiet
  • Avoid caffeine within 6 to 8 hours prior to going to bed
  • Avoid alcohol before bed
  • Get regular exercise, but not too close to bedtime
