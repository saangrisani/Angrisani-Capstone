A 5-Minute Grounding Exercise

  1. Look for 5 things you can see. Name them slowly.
  2. Touch 4 things you can feel (chair, floor, fabric, air).
  3. Listen for 3 sounds (near → far).
  4. Smell 2 scents (or recall ones you like).
  5. Breathe in for 4, hold 4, out for 6 — 5 times.

Tip: Pair this with a brief walk or stretch.
