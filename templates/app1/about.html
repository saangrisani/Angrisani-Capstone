About Veterans Companion

Veterans Companion is a student capstone project designed to help Veterans and families quickly find mental-health resources, facilities, and practical self-help tools.

Our Mission

Make it easier to discover VA and community care, understand benefits, and take simple steps toward better well-being—without the maze of tabs and forms.

What This Is / Isn’t
  • ✅ A guide to resources and helpful tools.
  • ✅ A way to locate nearby VA facilities.
  • ❌ Not medical advice; not a crisis service.
  • ❌ Not an official VA application.

Privacy Policy (Summary)

This site does not store personal data beyond what is needed for authentication and basic operation. Chat messages are processed by an AI service but are not stored.

If you have questions, contact the developer. This is a student project and not affiliated with the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs.
